Our Services

Comprehensive Industrial Solutions

🔧

Onsite Line Boring

Professional line boring services performed at your location. We restore worn bores to original specifications, ensuring precise tolerances and extended equipment life. Perfect for excavators, loaders, and heavy earthmoving equipment.

⚙️

Excavator Pin & Bush Repair

Expert repair and replacement of excavator pins and bushes. We handle all sizes and models, ensuring proper fit and alignment. Our precision work extends the life of your equipment and reduces maintenance costs.

🚜

Heavy Earthmover Repair

Comprehensive repair services for all types of heavy earthmoving machinery. From structural repairs to component replacement, we keep your equipment operating at peak performance.

🔥

Welding & Fabrication

Professional welding and fabrication services for industrial equipment. We work with all materials including steel, aluminum, and specialized alloys. Custom fabrication available for unique requirements.

💧

Hydraulic Repair

Expert hydraulic system diagnosis and repair. We service pumps, cylinders, valves, and complete hydraulic systems. Onsite testing and repair to minimize equipment downtime.

🛠️

Onside Machine Maintenance

Preventive and corrective maintenance services performed at your site. Regular maintenance programs available to keep your machinery running efficiently and prevent costly breakdowns.
